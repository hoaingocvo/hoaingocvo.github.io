Everyday Representations

Hoai Ngoc Vo

Week 1: Laurel Schwulst, My website is a shifting house next to a river of knowledge. What could yours be?

Citation: Laurel Schwulst, “My website is a shifting house next to a river of knowledge.”

Take a photograph of each of the three (3) items. Write short responses (about 150 words for each) for each of the representations you’ve chosen. You might answer these questions: How accurate is this representation to the thing it is referring or representing? What has been filtered out? Does it contain misleading information or distortions? Is the representation successful in its function overall? Post the image and the text for each representation to your public facing web site. Submit the URL to the entries to complete the assignment.
